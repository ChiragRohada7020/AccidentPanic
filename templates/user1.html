Automatic car accident detection and notification

SO Smart detects car accidents using the internal sensors(Accelerometer and GPS) of your smartphone, and sends an emergency notification with you location to your pre selected emergency contacts. This allowing your contacts to send help as soon as possible.

Manual or Automatic Mode

SOSmart car accident service can be configured in manual mode or automatic mode.
Automatic mode: Whenever SOSmart detects you are in a moving vehicle, it will automatically turn on the crash detection monitoring, allowing you to absolutelly forget about the app. Recommended for people that frequently travels in a vehicle.
Manual mode: User must manually turn the monitoring system in the app. Recommended for people who rarely travels in a moving vehicle

Effective emergency notification

No matter what your emergency contacts are doing, SOSmart will start a loud alarm which will call their atention . This alarm will only happen if your contacts have SOSmart installed

Panic Button

For any other emergency SOSmart has a panic button which when pressed, sends an emergency notification with your location to emergency contacts. and associated institutions.

Algorithm based on real car crash data

Our professional and sofisticated algorithm has been designed using real car crash data from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration. Using this data our algorithm can differentiate when you drop your phone, brake too hard or had a minor accident avoiding false alarms

Find Nearby Hospitals

In emergencies receiving medical atention on time can make the difference. SOSmart no matter where in the world you are, will show you a list of nearby hospitals and show you directions to get there as quick as possible

Real time monitoring platform for institutions



SOSmart offers a web platform which allows institutions to monitor emergencies 24 hours a day, 7 days a week anywhere in the world.
